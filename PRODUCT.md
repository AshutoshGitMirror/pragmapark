# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Smart Car Drivers**: Navigating urban environments looking for available, reasonably priced parking slots quickly, managing reservations and wallet payments.
- **Parking Lot Owners / Operators**: Optimizing slot utilization, predicting occupancy, adjusting dynamic pricing, and tracking revenue share ledger entries.
- **City Planners / Admins**: Auditing system health, observing historical occupancy, running counterfactual simulations on the digital twin, and monitoring sensor reliability.

## Product Purpose
- Implement a 6-layer pipeline showcasing the integration of IoT, ML, Blockchain, RL, Digital Twin, and Actuator infrastructure. Provide real-time telemetry, verifiable transaction histories, and predictive models to improve urban parking efficiency.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Precise, technical, analytical.
- **Tone**: Professional, research-grade, confident.
- **3-word personality**: Analytical, robust, futuristic.

## Anti-references
- Minimalist consumer SaaS designs with excessive whitespace, low data density, and vague summaries.
- Hardcoded mock-heavy dashboards that hide technical inner workings behind generic graphs.

## Design Principles
- **Data Density and Precision**: Present high-fidelity real-time telemetry, ML prediction intervals, and blockchain hashes directly.
- **Trust via Transparency**: Expose internal variables (e.g., sensor raw readings, pricing neural agent weights, QMIX consensus) so users understand the system's decisions.
- **Industrial Integrity**: Maintain a clean, high-contrast dark visual aesthetic that feels like a mission control room or terminal interface rather than a consumer utility.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. High contrast ratios for charts, visual status indicators, and full keyboard accessibility for map/table controls.
